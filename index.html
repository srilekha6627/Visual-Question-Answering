Cognitive Inference Through Visual Analysis

T. Harshitha, Sk. Dil Nawaz Farida, N. Sahithi Sri, P. Shiromani, N. Srilekha

Guide: V.Asha


Our multimodal VQA model combines both textual and visual information to generate answers to questions about images. The model architecture consists of two primary components: a text encoder and an image encoder. The text encoder utilizes a pre-trained BERT model to encode the textual input, while the image encoder employs a pre-trained ViT model to encode the visual input. These en-codings are then fused to generate a joint representation of the input data. Finally, a linear layer with softmax activation is used to predict the probability distri-bution over possible answers.
